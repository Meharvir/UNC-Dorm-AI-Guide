# UNC Chapel Hill Dorms — Comprehensive Housing Guide (RAG-Style Document)

Carolina Housing – Key Facts and Policies

- Housing contract is for the full academic year (Fall + Spring) for most residence halls.
- 2025–2026 on-campus housing rates:
  - Double occupancy: $4,139 / semester → $8,278 / year
  - Triple/Quad occupancy: $4,016 / semester → $8,032 / year
  - Single occupancy: $4,902 / semester → $9,804 / year
  - Super Suites (Morrison): $4,580 / semester → $9,160 / year
- Rates include utilities, internet, laundry, and residential activities.
- Summer housing is available for some students.
- Summer cancellation: After key pickup, rent is prorated weekly for first 3 weeks; after that, full cost applies.

Contract cancellation policy:
- Cancel before December 15: $500 fee
- December 16 – January 16: pay 50% of Fall room rent if you cancel
- On or after January 17: owe prorated rent plus cancellation charges equal to 50% of remaining contract
- Housing Contract is legally binding

Community Living Standards (behavior policy):
- Violations can lead to written warnings, probation, or contract termination

Graduate / Family Housing — Baity Hill
- Baity Hill is UNC’s apartment community for graduate students and student-families
- Apartments offer:
  - Utilities included (water, electricity, internet)
  - Full kitchen, dishwasher, washer/dryer connections
  - 1-bedroom & 2-bedroom layouts
- Contract term at Baity Hill: 12-month lease (not just academic year) for many residents
- Cancellation / contract terms are detailed in Baity Hill’s specific contract

Housing Conduct & Behavioral Policies
- Carolina Housing enforces a Housing Conduct Process
- If students break rules (noise, prohibited items, etc.) they may receive:
  - Written Warning for first-time minor offenses
  - Probation (6–12 months) for serious or repeated violations
  - Contract Termination for severe misconduct (loss of on-campus housing)

Flexible Contract / Study Abroad Info
- Flexible housing contracts for students planning to study abroad or do off-campus internships
- Proof of study abroad waives cancellation fee
- Returning students: cancellation policy and deadlines are clearly defined

Move-In Information
- Fall 2025 move-in begins August 13 for new and returning residents
- Students must follow proper check-out procedures when leaving, especially if canceling or moving out early

Other Useful Resources
- Off-Campus Housing: UNC provides tools and legal resources for students living off campus
- Contract Cancellation Timeline Graphic: Carolina Housing publishes detailed cancellation deadlines and costs
